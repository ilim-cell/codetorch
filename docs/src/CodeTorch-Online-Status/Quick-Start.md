# Quick Start - CodeTorch Online Status Detector

## How to view Wafflez Studios online status
By default, the project is set to show the online status of Wafflez Studios. To view this status, simply open the project and click the flag icon. The system will fetch the cloud data and display whether Wafflez Studios is online or offline.

## How to use this for your own account
It is easy to set this up for your own account. You will need to remix the project and set it up with your own CodeTorch account. Follow these steps:
1. Remix the project by clicking the "Remix" button.
2. Click "Setup" and click through the information slides.
3. Accept the MIT license terms and conditions.
4. Share the project to make it public.
5. Open the project every time you log on to CodeTorch to ensure that the status is updated correctly. If you do not open the project, the status may not reflect your actual online presence. You may continue to use CodeTorch as normal in another background tab.

> [!NOTE]
> All instructions are in the project information slides, so please ensure to read it correctly.

> [!TIP]
> Press Q to mute or unmute the music.
