# CodeTorch Online Status Detector

The CodeTorch Online Status Detector is a tool that allows your users to monitor the online status of your CodeTorch profile. It provides real-time updates on whether your profile is online or offline, helping you ensure that your users are informed with transparency. This tool is particularly useful for developers, content creators, and anyone who wants to maintain a consistent online presence.

The project allows you to maintain a verifiable online status simply by opening and running the project in the background. It also allows you to manually set a status message to let your users know what you are currently doing, should there be any disruptions.

The project is easily customsizable, allowing you to change the colors, text, backgrounds, music, and other elements to match your personal or brand style. You can also add your own custom messages to provide additional context to your users.

> [!WARNING]
> - If you are not logged in, the project will not work or may show inaccurate data. Cloud variables are required to fetch status from CodeTorch servers and only signed-in CodeTorch users can fetch from the API. Please ensure that you are logged in to your CodeTorch account before using this tool.
> - If you are the owner of the SDK, you need to open the project every time you log on to CodeTorch to ensure that the status is updated correctly. If you do not open the project, the status may not reflect your actual online presence.
