# Quick Start - AI Detector

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you write in US English, make sure to enable the toggle in the bottom right corner. Do not enable toggle if US English is not your primary language.

1. Click Press to enter text.
2. Type or paste the text you want to check.
3. Press Enter or click ✅ to see the result.
4. (Optional) Hold down WHY? to view the reasoning behind the classification.

> [!WARNING]
> No AI detector is 100% accurate. AI Detector is a tool that uses linguistic analysis to make an informed prediction about whether a piece of text was generated by an AI model or written by a human. However, it is important to note that the results are not definitive and should be used as a guide rather than a final judgment. Do not rely on AI Detector as the sole means of determining the origin of a text, and always consider other factors and evidence when making conclusions about authorship.
