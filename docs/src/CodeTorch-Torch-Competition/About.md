# WAFFLEZ26 TORCHER COMPETITION

Welcome to the **WAFFLEZ**26 Torcher Competition!
This event is designed to raise awareness and recognition for projects that are unknown, exceptionally high quality, or completely unique.

While many creators push the limits of Scratch, we are here to push the advanced boundaries of CodeTorch, which has significantly higher performance capabilities.



## 🚀 PARTICIPATE
1. Click through the project slides to view information about how to submit
2. Remix this project and craft something entirely on your own using your favourite environment. (Make sure to delete the announcement slides/code before building to keep your project 100% clean of external code).
3. Set the visibility to "Public" and publish it. There is no need to comment a link or tag the project, as all submissions will be automatically collected.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> ALL SUBMISSIONS MUST BE RECIEVED BY 28TH AUGUST 2026.

## 🎁 WIN
Winners will receive custom profile shoutouts, technical breakdowns, and likes/loves. More details are available inside the project!

All entries that follow the rules and score at least 40 points will automatically receive like and love on their submission.
