# Quick Start - Life in Three Moves

## Start the simulation
Press the green button in the top left corner of the screen to start the simulation. You can pause and resume at any time.

## 🎮 How it works
- Three species, one loop — Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.
- Autonomous agents — Each creature moves toward its prey and away from its predator.
- Equal speed, random starts — No species has an advantage; balance emerges from chaos.
- Repopulation bursts — Every win spawns 20 new rocks, papers, and scissors, keeping the world alive and in motion.
- High‑FPS simulation — The system evolves faster than you can predict, creating patterns that feel organic and alive.
