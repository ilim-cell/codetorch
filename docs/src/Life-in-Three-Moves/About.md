# 🌱 Life in Three Moves
Life in Three Moves is a living, breathing ecosystem built from the simplest game ever taught: rock, paper, scissors. What begins as 10 rocks, 10 papers, and 10 scissors quickly unfolds into a world of motion, colour, and surprising complexity.

Each species chases the one it can defeat and flees the one that can defeat it. Every encounter reshapes the landscape. Every victory triggers a new wave of life. The result is a constantly shifting tapestry of spirals, blooms, collapses, and rebirths; a tiny universe that never repeats the same moment twice.

> [!CAUTION]
> This project is laggy and may crash your browser. It is not recommended to run it on mobile devices or low-end computers with limited RAM.
> This project uses a workaround to create unlimited clones, which can cause performance issues. If you experience lag, disable "Infinite Clones" in the settings menu or reduce RAM usage.

## 🌍 What this project shows

Life in Three Moves is a reminder that creativity doesn’t need complexity. With just three rules and a bit of motion, anyone can build a world that feels alive. It’s a perfect example of what CodeTorch is about: simple tools, big ideas, and the joy of watching something unexpected emerge from your own imagination.
