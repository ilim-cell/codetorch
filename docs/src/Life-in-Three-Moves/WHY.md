## 🎨 Why it feels alive
Even though the rules are tiny, the world they create is anything but. The simulation forms:

- swirling color vortices
- expanding dominance waves
- chaotic clusters that dissolve and reform
- rhythmic pulses of new life

Every frame looks like a snapshot of a living reef, a weather system, or a microscopic organism mid growth.

> *"It's beautifully messy and that's the point. The chaos is the art."*
