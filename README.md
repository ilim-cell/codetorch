# Wafflez Studios CodeTorch community forum
Welcome to the Wafflez community forum! Official community issue tracking and bug reporting campus for [Wafflez Studios](https://codetorch.net/users/Wafflez_Wif_Syrup) projects.

Wafflez Studios' mission is to pioneer sustainable digital projects for a modern and united world. On CodeTorch, we are the creators of PolyOS and ScratchGPT, who build complex virtualization architectures out of blocks for the sport of it.
